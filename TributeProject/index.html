Alexander (Technoblade)

"Technoblade never dies!"

1999-2022

Biography

Alexander, also known as Technoblade, is an American Youtuber who was well-known for their Minecraft videos and livestreams. His Youtube channel grew at around 10.5m subscribers before his passing. At the age of 23 in June 2022, he died from Metastatic Sarcoma.
